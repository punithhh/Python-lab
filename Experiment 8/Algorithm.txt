PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Implement ‘re’ module in python

ALGORITHM:
Step 1 : Start the program.
Step 2 : Import the re module.
Step 3 : Accept an email text from the user.
Step 4 : Use re.match() to check whether the text starts with letters.
Step 5 : Use re.search() to find @ symbol.
Step 6 : Use re.findall() to find all letters and digits.
Step 7 : Use re.split() to split the text at @.
Step 8 : Use re.sub() to replace . with _.
Step 9 : Use re.finditer() to show positions of digits.
Step 10 : Display all results.
Step 11 : Stop the program.
